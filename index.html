Biography


I am currently a full-time post-doc researcher in project ANTAREX (AutoTuning and Adaptivity appRoach for Energy efficient eXascale HPC systems).
Prior to this, I was a post-doctoral researcher in Irish Centre for Cloud Computing and Commerce (IC4).

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP), Portugal in 2016, under the supervision of Professor Jorge G. Barbosa.

My research interests include Distributed and Parallel Computing, Resource Allocation, Cloud Computing, Auto-Scaling in cloud environment.
