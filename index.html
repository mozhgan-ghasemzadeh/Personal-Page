Biography


Joined the Brunel University London as a postdoc in December 2018.


Before joining Brunel, I worked as a full-time post-doc researcher in project ANTAREX (AutoTuning and Adaptivity appRoach for Energy efficient eXascale HPC systems). Prior to this, I was a post-doctoral researcher in Irish Center for Cloud Computing and Commerce (IC4).


I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP), Portugal.


My research interests include Distributed and Parallel Computing, Resource Allocation, Cloud Computing, Auto-Scaling in cloud environment, and compiler.
